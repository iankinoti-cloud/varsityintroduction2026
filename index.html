Our Philosophy

I am because we are

At Merishaw School, we believe in the Ubuntu philosophy—the idea that our humanity is deeply interconnected. Every achievement, every lesson, and every act of kindness is a thread in the fabric of our community. Here, we nurture not just scholars, but compassionate leaders who understand that true greatness is found in lifting others as we rise.

Meet Our Mascot

The Merishaw Shield

A beacon of courage, unity, and the spirit of Ubuntu.

Our mascot, stands as a proud guardian at the heart of Merishaw. More than a symbol, it represents the unwavering strength that comes from standing together—protecting, uplifting, and inspiring every student to lead with compassion and honor.

With the heart of a lion and the wisdom of the morans, The Shield reminds us that true greatness is forged in community. At Merishaw, every victory is shared, every challenge faced as one, and every student is empowered to become a champion for others.
