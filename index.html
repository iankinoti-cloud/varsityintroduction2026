Discover Morans' Athletics

The Merishaw Way: "Ubuntu"

At Merishaw School, sports are more than just games; they are the truest expression of our core philosophy: Ubuntu. This ancient African word reminds us that our humanity is tied to one another.

When a Merishaw Lion steps onto the field, they do not play for personal glory. They play for the student sitting in the bleachers, the teacher in the classroom, and the teammate standing beside them. We rise together, we face challenges together, and we triumph together.

"A person is a person through other persons. We are MORANS."

Meet The Merishaw Mascot

While we are fiercely known as the Morans, our unique mascot—The Merishaw Shield—represents the heart of our community. Built tough, smiling proudly, and forged in our signature maroon, the Shield is the living embodiment of our defense and solidarity.

The Shield reminds every athlete and student that our greatest strength is how we protect and support one another. It takes a Lion's heart and a unified Shield to win the day.

Earning your stripes. Wearing the Maroon and Cream.

The Varsity Standard

To wear the Merishaw varsity jacket is to carry the weight and pride of our entire history on your shoulders. The iconic maroon body and cream sleeves are recognized statewide not just for athletic dominance, but for unparalleled sportsmanship. Earning the 'M' means you have proven your dedication to the team above yourself.
